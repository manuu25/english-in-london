Buddy Program

Get paired with a local London student or someone who's been here longer. Your buddy will help you navigate the city, practice English in real situations, and become your first friend in London. It's like having a local best friend from day one.

  • ✓ One-on-one matching based on interests and goals
  • ✓ Weekly meetups for conversation practice
  • ✓ London exploration and cultural exchange
